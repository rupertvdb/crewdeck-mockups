Design rationale (mockup chrome — not shipped)

Hero asymmetry, not grid uniformity. AI Filmmaker takes a 2:1 landscape "still" — the headliner. Pitchmaker is a square co-feature. Distribution + Archive are quiet sleeves: visible, dated, deliberately unlit. The brief's cardinal sin — four equal cards — is killed at the layout level, not with bigger icons.

Typography pairing. Inter only (CrewDeck token), but used at three weights and a 5x size range — display-scale serial titles (44–56px, weight 500, tight tracking) against utility 13px metadata. The contrast is the typography, not a second font.

Imagery as identity. Every app and every recent-work tile gets a multi-radial gradient "still" — five distinct colour moods, never the purple-pink default. No 16px Lucide icons anywhere near a title. Film grain overlay on Variant B's hero only.

Rejected: glassmorphism, generic SaaS card grids, dark-mode-everywhere, gradient text, animated blobs, purple→pink. Kept restrained: one drifting hero, staggered fade-in on load, hover lifts only on actionable cards.
