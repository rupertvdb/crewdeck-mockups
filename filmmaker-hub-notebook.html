A note on the setting

The brief asked for a film bible, not a dashboard. Type does the work here: Newsreader for headlines and prose (a contemporary serif drawn for screens, with an italic that actually whispers), set against IBM Plex Sans and Plex Mono for captions, folios and ordinal numbers — three voices, one publication.

Rejected: Playfair (too wedding-invitation), Inter alone (too SaaS), and every instinct to put an icon next to a title. Colour appears three times — ink, muted ink, and a single blue reserved for live actions. Hierarchy comes from weight, italic and white space.

The move I'm proudest of: projects and tools share one indexed list grammar — numbered, leaded, hairline-ruled — so a documentary in progress and the software making it sit on the same shelf. No card-with-icon anywhere on the page.

— Design note · v1
